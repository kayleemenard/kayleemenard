Redesign of Mr Potato Head Toy Packaging

Fall 2014

Goal: Redesign a toy's packaging and using eco-pakaging materials

How: After researching the long history of Mr. Potato Head and friends, I found out that Hasbro had never presented the famous toy in a potato sack! I decided it was an ideal design for this challenge because of it's novelty as well for it's practicality for reusing as the toy's many accessory container, it's repurposing potential, and it's uses for composting of recycling.
