Logo Design for Contract Welder

TASK: Create logo for independant welder and farm equipment repairman, in addition create stationary and brand guide.

I was fortunate to be given full creative freedom over the project. I aimed to create a brand that would be unique among the clients competition—without stepping outside of the norm for branding expectations within the trades—be considerate of the clients budget constraints as a small contractor, and take his clients interests into account.
